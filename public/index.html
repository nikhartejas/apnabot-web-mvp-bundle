FAQs

Do I need new hardware or apps?
No. ApnaBot runs on your existing WhatsApp Business and browser. Optional links to Vyapar/myBillBook.
Which languages are supported?
English, Hindi, and Hinglish by default. We can adapt to Marathi/Bengali/Tamil for key flows.
Can humans take over the chat?
Always. Your staff/owner can jump in anytime. ApnaBot is designed for human‑in‑the‑loop control.
What’s the typical setup time?
One day for the quick win (auto replies + reviews). Advanced flows roll out over 2–4 weeks.
